Web design fundamentals

Posted by Tim Dalton 2 Days Ago (4 Comments)

In order to put your creativity to the real test, web designers will have to now design websites that not only appeal to the bigger screens but also to the small portable devices. While providing a good design is essential, making it usable and not very blotchy is more important, clean design is what it is reffered to today. Mobile first, Responsive and thin web applications are the requirements most business owners ask for while designing websites. These terms make little sense to seasoned designers who were paid to provide excellent visual appeal using heavy frameworks like Flash and the like.
